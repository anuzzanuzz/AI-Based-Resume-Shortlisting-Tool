About Our Project

The AI-Based Resume Shortlisting Tool is designed to automate the recruitment process using Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing. It extracts skills and experience from resumes and compares them with job descriptions using TF-IDF and Cosine Similarity, generating ranked results for recruiters.

This project reduces manual screening time, ensures fair evaluation, and brings smarter hiring decisions.
